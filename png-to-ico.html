How to Convert Images to ICO for Favicon in Seconds

Generate crisp ICO favicons from PNGs directly in your browser—centered, resized to 256×256, and ready for production.

Favicons are the tiny icons browsers show in tabs and bookmarks. The traditional format is ICO. Here’s how to create a pixel-perfect favicon in seconds.

Steps

  1. Open the converter and add your logo PNG.
  2. Choose ICO (256×256) as the output format.
  3. Click Convert, then download your .ico file.

Tips for best results

  • Start from a square PNG with plenty of margin around the logo.
  • Avoid tiny text; keep shapes bold and simple.
  • Use a transparent background for clean edges.

Integrate in your site

<link rel="icon" href="/favicon.ico" sizes="any">

That’s all—your favicon is production‑ready.
